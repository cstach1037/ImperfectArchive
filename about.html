What do I mean by 'an imperfect archive?'

No archive is neutral, and by proxy, no archive will be perfect in its encompassing of a given history, a life. This archive is no exception.

  1. This website features a highly-curated collection of archival photos and video that have yet to find their place in my portfolio.
  2. Certain absences (people, periods) reflect the messiness of an archive—and of consistently documenting your teenage years.
  3. 'Archiving' is a constant process, one that's always in flux. Conceptually, this project is a work-in-progress.
